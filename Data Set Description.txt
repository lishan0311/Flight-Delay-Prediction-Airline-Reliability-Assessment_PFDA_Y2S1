Data Set Description (flights)

1 CONTEXT:

The U.S. Department of Transportation's (DOT) Bureau of Transportation Statistics tracks the on-time performance of domestic flights operated by large air carriers. Summary information on the number of on-time, delayed, canceled, and diverted flights is published in DOT's monthly Air Travel Consumer Report and in this dataset of 2015 flight delays and cancellations.


2 METADATA:

    2.1 Flight Details

	FLIGHT_NUMBER: A unique identifier for each flight.
	TAIL_NUMBER: A unique identifier for each aircraft.
	AIRLINE: A unique identifier for each airline (REFER TO IATA_AIRLINE_CODES for airline name).
	ORIGIN_AIRPORT: Starting airport (REFER TO IATA_AIRPORT_CODES for airport name).
	DESTINATION_AIRPORT: Destination airport (REFER TO IATA_AIRPORT_CODES for airport name).
	DISTANCE: Distance between two airports.

    2.2 Flight Cancellations and Diversions

	DIVERTED: Aircraft landed on airport different from original destination airport (0: No; 1: Yes).
	CANCELLED: Flight cancelled (0: not cancelled; 1: cancelled).
	CANCELLATION_REASON: Reason for cancellation of flight. 
		     	     (A: Airline/Carrier; 
		      	      B: Weather; 
  	              	      C: National Air System; 
		      	      D: Security)

    2.3 Flight Timings

	SCHEDULED_TIME: Planned time required for each flight.
	SCHEDULED_DEPARTURE: Planned departure time.
	SCHEDULED_ARRIVAL: Planned arrival time.
	WHEELS_ON: Time point that the aircraft's wheels touch the ground.
	WHEELS_OFF: Time point that the aircraft's wheels leave the ground.
	AIR_TIME: Time duration between WHEELS_OFF and WHEELS_ON time.
	TAXI_IN: Time duration elapsed between WHEELS_ON and gate arrival at DESTINATION_AIRPORT.
	TAXI_OUT: The time duration elapsed between departure from the ORIGIN_AIRPORT and WHEELS_OFF.
	ARRIVAL_TIME: WHEELS_ON + TAXI_IN.
	DEPARTURE_TIME: WHEEL_OFF - TAXI_OUT.
	ELAPSED_TIME: AIR_TIME + TAXI_IN + TAXI_OUT

    2.4 Flight Delays

	ARRIVAL_DELAY: ARRIVAL_TIME - SCHEDULED_ARRIVAL
	DEPARTURE_DELAY: Total delay on departure.
	AIR_SYSTEM_DELAY: Delay caused by air system.
	SECURITY_DELAY: Delay caused by security.
	AIRLINE_DELAY: Delay caused by AIRLINE.
	LATE_AIRCRAFT_DELAY: Delay caused by aircraft.
	WEATHER_DELAY: Delay caused by weather.

    2.5 Date and Time Information

	YEAR: The year of the flight trip.
	MONTH: The month of the flight trip.
	DAY: The day of the flight trip.
	DAY_OF_WEEK: Day of the week when the flight occurs.
